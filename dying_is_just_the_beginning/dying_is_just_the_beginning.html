Introduction

You awake in a cell it is damp and gloomy. As you look around you can see a strange light shining from one of the walls, it is just sheading enough light for you to see your surroundings but not much else. As you go to get up you realise that you are on some form of alter in the middle of the cell. As you look around your cell you realise there are others with you.
