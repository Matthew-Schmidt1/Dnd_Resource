The point of this campaign is to kill the party as much as you can. The more they
kill the more you reward them and the more they run away the less you reward them.

For fun Rewards, amazing deaths one hit Player kills or complete party kills.

When they do some thing the Evil God likes make them roll a CON save DC 10 to check
if they suffer damage (1D4) for when the Evil God interacts with them.

Unkown to the players this is the Domain of a evil God. He is keeping
them around for ammusement. By reviving them and everything in the dungeon.

Rewards can be levels or magical items. The magic items should be Soul
bound as it will just make it easier for the Part.

Most Items that the players pick up in the dungeon will not go to there next life
only Soul Bound items will transfer to the next life (GM decides what is Soul Bound)
.

Soul Bound : Term used for this campaign it means a item that will carry
over with the player on there death. If the GM is nice they can make quest
objects Soul bound or make them return to there orginal state.

Example: the last quest is to get all for hearts of the Dragons
and go to the center of the Gambling Pit where there are a
set of stairs going up and down. GM can make the hearts disapear
and the dragons reappear on death.
